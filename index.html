About Me

I am a UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer graduated from Universitas Negeri Surabaya with a degree in Information Technology Education. I focus on creating digital products that are functional, intuitive, and visually appealing.

My workflow bridges design and technology. I translate Figma prototypes into clean, responsive code using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS. Thanks to my software engineering internship, I also possess foundational knowledge in Git, databases, and servers.

I actively sharpen my skills by building dummy projects, UI redesigns, and application prototypes used as learning models for vocational (SMK) students. Detail-oriented and highly adaptable, I am ready to bring my technical and design skills to a professional team.
